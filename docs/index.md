# About

effy is a terminal FFmpeg helper that simplifies common tasks. It's small and friendly, that's why it's named eFFy — FFmpeg's diminutive form.

![effy TUI](assets/main.gif#only-dark)
![effy TUI](assets/mainw.gif#only-light)

## Features

- change a video resolution, bitrate, frame rate, speed
- change an audio bitrate, volume, pitch, tempo
- apply audio effects (crystalizer)
- extract or remove audio from video
- trim video or audio
- use hardware acceleration
- apply [presets](usage.md#presets) (preload in the UI, or immediately from the CLI)
