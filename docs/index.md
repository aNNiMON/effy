# About

effy is a terminal FFmpeg helper that simplifies common tasks. It's small and friendly, that's why it's named eFFy â€” FFmpeg's diminutive form.

## Features

- change a video resolution, bitrate, frame rate, speed
- change an audio bitrate, volume, pitch, tempo
- apply audio effects (crystalizer)
- extract or remove audio from video
- trim video or audio
